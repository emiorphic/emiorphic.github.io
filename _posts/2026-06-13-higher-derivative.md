# Notations
Scalar quantities (elements of $$\mathbb{R}$$) are written normally, while vector quantities (elements of $$\mathbb{R}^2$$ and above) are written in upright boldface. Matrices are written in Sans-Serif (e.g. $$\mathsf{M}$$)
